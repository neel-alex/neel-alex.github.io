Grub Arithmetic Trainer

46 grubs have been trapped! For some weird but diegetically-plausible reason, you can only save them by solving math problems!

Practice saving the grubs! In this mode, you'll be shown some math problems, but there's no penalty to failing. You'll get to see a full report on your progress at the end.

Go out into the world and save the grubs for yourself! Careful though, if you make one mistake, the Collector will seize on your error and trap them again! Still, if you save them all, and manage to do it fast enough, there's a special reward at the end...
